Technology
I am very involved with Open Source Computing, as well as Linux Development. I am a frequent aide on UbuntuForums as well as Launchpad.net and various IRC chats.

My Operating System of Choice is Ubuntu, not only for its ease of use, but for its compatibility with Windows and Mac systems. Recently, I began using Arch Linux on my netbook, and am very impressed with its ability to utilize the limited resources found on the device. I operate and maintain several groups of computers, each set up for specific tasks.

Currently, I maintain 4 computers, all of which are serviceable from my Android cell phone anywhere in the world. 

I have also taught myself circuits, and have created several hobby applications using an Arduino board. I have some information regarding small projects that I have completed below.

I have been featured or have had adaptations of my projects featured on several prominent and respectable websites, including Lifehacker and TechCrunch.
